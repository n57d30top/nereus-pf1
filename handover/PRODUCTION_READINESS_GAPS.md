# Production Readiness Gaps

## Current State

`PF-1` is **concept-ready**, not **production-ready**.

## Main Missing Layers

### 1. Material Instantiation

We still do not have a finalized real material definition suitable for production transfer.

### 2. Bench-Scale Validation

We still need real-world measurements for:

- capture performance
- fouling behavior
- regeneration recovery
- pressure-drop behavior
- breakthrough/service window

### 3. Matrix Robustness

The bounded internal model still needs translation into real stressed wastewater matrices with competing ions, organic load, and surfactant burden.

### 4. Packaging Reality

The current module framing is strong, but cartridge geometry, media packing, support layers, and replacement/regeneration logistics are still concept-level.

### 5. Economic Reality

Cost-per-volume and service-cycle economics remain planning assumptions, not evidence.

## What "Production" Should Mean Here

In this handover, "production" should first mean:

- identify a plausible material family
- produce initial candidate material samples
- test them under bounded PFAS-relevant conditions
- decide quickly whether `PF-1` survives contact with reality

Not:

- scale-up commitment
- field deployment
- product launch
